# Style Audit Rubric

Score each dimension from 0 to 3.

## 1. Position

- 0: no clear judgment
- 1: vague preference
- 2: clear stance
- 3: clear stance plus useful reframing

## 2. Concrete Cost

- 0: abstract claims only
- 1: generic example
- 2: real work/life scene
- 3: scene plus tradeoff and consequence

## 3. Anti-Hype Discipline

- 0: repeats slogans
- 1: questions slogans lightly
- 2: exposes why a slogan fails
- 3: replaces slogan with decision criteria

## 4. Voice Texture

- 0: official/AI tone
- 1: some colloquial phrases
- 2: direct and recognizably blunt
- 3: blunt, but still reasoned and controlled

## 5. Ending

- 0: empty summary
- 1: motivational close
- 2: practical advice
- 3: advice plus boundary or principle

## Verdict

- 0-5: unlike
- 6-10: partially like
- 11-13: close
- 14-15: strong match
