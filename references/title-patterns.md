# Title Patterns

## Major Patterns

### 1. Direct Rebuttal

Used when attacking hype, false consensus, or naive advice.

Templates:

- `别扯了，{流行说法}从来都不是{真问题}`
- `醒醒吧，{你以为的答案}可能只是{陷阱}`
- `{对象}不是{光鲜标签}，而是{真实成本}`

### 2. Rhetorical Question

Used to make the reader self-diagnose.

Templates:

- `{现象}，你真的想明白了吗？`
- `为什么{群体}总是{行为/误区}？`
- `{选择A}，还是{选择B}？`

### 3. Personal Confession

Used for reboot, career, writing, low point, or life topics.

Templates:

- `{时间段}，我才想明白{一件事}`
- `我也曾{错误/低谷}，但后来发现{真相}`
- `{经历}之后，我开始{改变}`

### 4. Case-Based Diagnosis

Used for technical management, architecture, interviews, or organizational issues.

Templates:

- `经验：为什么你{管理/技术动作}越来越难？`
- `当{场景}发生时，你的{团队/系统/职业}会怎样？`
- `{系统/团队}是不是{表面能力}，就看能否解决这几个问题`

### 5. Mocking Hype

Use sparingly. The target must be a public illusion, not a vulnerable person.

Templates:

- `{热词}突然又火了，它到底是不是骗局？`
- `少扯蛋！{口号}绝不是{真实分水岭}`
- `{伪专业姿态}，可能只是别人眼中的幼稚与荒唐`

## Title Checklist

- Is there a concrete conflict?
- Does the title imply a judgment?
- Would the title still work if stripped of buzzwords?
- Is the sharpness backed by the article body?
- Does it avoid clickbait that the content cannot pay off?
